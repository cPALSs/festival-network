# Greater Sacramento — Lunar New Year Season Landscape

**Purpose:** Map **Lunar New Year, Tết, and Spring Festival events** in Greater Sacramento — the competitive set and **season network** for **cPALSs Lunar New Year Tết Festival** (Elk Grove Regional Park capstone).

**Scope:** Greater Sacramento only. **January–March** window. Attendance figures marked **‡** = organizer/press claim unless CCSD or third-party count confirmed.

**Live rows:** [Competitive landscape Google Sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1IB6beiKHC9R05r2o0imkh8MX_2u-qmhlVxTV3pxibs0/edit)

**Method:** Seeded from 2026 **Lunar New Year Season** flyer (12 dated events Jan 24–Mar 7) + cPALSs research docs. Flyer source file not preserved in repo.

**Last updated:** June 2026

---

## 1. Executive summary

| Finding | Implication |
|---------|-------------|
| **LNY is a season, not a weekend** | At least **12 public events** listed on the 2026 season flyer before/after the Elk Grove capstone |
| **Feb 7 cluster** | SCNCCA, Creative Space, and Arden Fair × Chamber all land **one week before** capstone — warm-up, not substitute |
| **Feb 14 collision day** | Capstone + District56 street fair + Locke heritage + Vietnamese Martyrs evening fair — families choose **time slots**, not one host |
| **Honesty gap on our capstone** | Marketed **10K+** vs CCSD **5–6K** — same pattern as AAPI Night Market **4K marketed vs ~2K field** |
| **Twin season bridge** | Mardi Gras parades (City of Trees, SacTown) sit **late Feb–Mar** — roll-up opportunity named in 2026 fusion narrative |
